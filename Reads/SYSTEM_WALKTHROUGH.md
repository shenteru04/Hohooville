
# Hohoo-ville System 7-Minute Walkthrough

**Explanation:**
This document provides a concise, step-by-step walkthrough of the Hohoo-ville Training Management System. It is designed to help new users, trainees, trainers, registrars, and admins quickly understand the overall workflow, the responsibilities of each role, and how the system guides users from account creation to training completion and certification. Use this as a quick orientation or onboarding reference for anyone involved in the system.

## 1. Introduction (0:00-0:30)
- Welcome to the Hohoo-ville Training Management System.
- This platform streamlines the process from user account creation to training completion and certification.

## 2. Admin Role (0:30-1:30)
- The Admin logs in and manages all user accounts.
- Admin creates accounts for registrars, trainers, and trainees.
- Admin oversees the system setup, ensuring all roles are in place.
- Admin can archive or deactivate users at the end of their training lifecycle.

## 3. Registrar Role (1:30-3:00)
- Registrar logs in to set up the training structure.
- Creates qualifications/courses based on training needs.
- Adds trainers and assigns them to specific qualifications.
- Creates batches (groups of trainees) and sets their schedules.
- Opens the application form for trainees to apply.
- Reviews submitted applications, approves or rejects them, and assigns approved trainees to batches.

## 4. Trainer Role (3:00-4:30)
- Trainer logs in and sees their assigned batches and qualifications.
- Prepares modules, lessons, and tasks for each batch.
- Schedules lessons and posts learning materials, quizzes, and tasks.
- Conducts training sessions and monitors trainee progress.
- Grades trainees based on their performance and task completion.

## 5. Trainee Role (4:30-6:00)
- Trainee logs in and fills out the application form.
- Waits for approval from the registrar and batch assignment.
- Once approved, attends training sessions and completes assigned modules and tasks.
- Receives notifications for new lessons, tasks, and grades.
- At the end of training, receives a certificate if requirements are met.

## 6. End-of-Training and User Lifecycle (6:00-7:00)
- After training, admin or registrar can generate certificates for successful trainees.
- Users who have completed their roles can be archived or deactivated for data retention and compliance.
- The system maintains an audit trail for all archival and deactivation actions.

## 7. Summary (7:00)
- Hohoo-ville provides a seamless, role-based workflow from system setup to training completion.
- Each role has clear responsibilities, and the system ensures accountability, transparency, and efficiency throughout the training process.
