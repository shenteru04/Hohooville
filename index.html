About Hohoo Ville Technical School

We are a premier technical-vocational institution dedicated to providing high-quality, hands-on training to empower our students for a competitive global market. Our state-of-the-art facilities and expert trainers ensure you get the best education possible.

Located in Purok 6A, Poblacion, Lagonglong, Misamis Oriental, we are committed to fostering a learning environment that promotes innovation, skill, and professionalism.

Our Featured Courses

We offer a range of TESDA-accredited programs.

Electrical Installation & Maintenance (EIM) NC II

Learn to install and maintain electrical wiring, lighting, and related equipment for residential and commercial buildings.

Automotive Servicing NC II

Gain expertise in inspecting, maintaining, and repairing light and heavy-duty automotive vehicles.

Cookery NC II

Master the art of preparing hot and cold meals in commercial kitchens, from appetizers to desserts.
